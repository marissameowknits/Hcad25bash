Silent Auction

HCAD will host a silent auction during the bash to raise money to support Maryland School for the Deaf-Columbia students in attending summer camps and other programs. 100% of the proceeds from the silent auction will go directly towards funding these opportunities for deaf children. Your participation and contributions to the auction will help provide valuable opportunities for these kids.
